# Raashi Corpus — Characteristics of the 12 Moon Signs

This is based on the moon sign (Janma Raashi) of the birth chart — not the sun sign.

---

## Mesham or Mesh (Aries)
**Element:** Fire | **Lord:** Mars | **Friends Raashi:** Meenam, Dhanu, Simha

Mesh rashi people are dynamic, courageous, and pioneers in their fields. As the first sign of the zodiac, it embodies a youthful enthusiasm and a zest for life. Mesh makes you a natural leader, eager to take the initiative and embrace new challenges. It endows you with a warrior-like determination and a competitive edge. Being a Mesh enables you to be straightforward — this can make you appear blunt, but it also makes you honest and transparent. Mesh lets you thrive in environments where you can take charge and set the pace, and you excel in roles that require decisiveness and action.

This also means Mesh can make you impatient, wanting immediate results and becoming frustrated with delays or obstacles. This impatience can manifest in personal life, where you may struggle to tolerate different viewpoints. In terms of habits, Mesh rashi makes you active and enjoy physical activities. You have a competitive streak with an enthusiasm for sports. Mesh rashi also lends you independence and self-reliance, sometimes making you resist asking for help.

**Key traits:** Courage, independence, leadership, impulsiveness, energy, ambition.
**Career:** Military, sports, entrepreneurship, medicine, engineering.

---

## Rishabam or Vrishabha (Taurus)
**Element:** Earth | **Lord:** Venus | **Friends Raashi:** Mithunam, Rishaba

Rishabha rashi people are stable, patient, and deeply devoted to comfort and beauty. They have a strong love for material pleasures — good food, fine arts, music, and nature. They are reliable and trustworthy, and once they commit to something, they follow through with great persistence. They are warm and affectionate but can be possessive in relationships.

Rishabha natives are slow to anger but formidable once provoked — like the bull, they are patient but powerful. They have excellent practical sense and are skilled at accumulating wealth over time. They dislike change and can become stubborn or resistant to new ideas. They value security and stability above all.

**Key traits:** Patience, determination, love of beauty, sensuality, stubbornness, practicality.
**Career:** Finance, banking, arts, music, food industry, real estate.

---

## Mithunam or Mithuna (Gemini)
**Element:** Air | **Lord:** Mercury | **Friends Raashi:** Mithunam, Dhanusu, Meena, Thulam, Rishaba

Mithuna rashi people are intellectual, communicative, and adaptable. They are quick thinkers with a gift for language and versatile interests. They are curious, social, and enjoy variety in life. They are charming and witty, making them excellent communicators and networkers. Their dual nature means they can sometimes appear inconsistent or two-faced, but this actually reflects their ability to see multiple perspectives.

Mithuna natives are playful and youthful even in old age. They have a short attention span and can get bored easily with routine. They thrive when intellectually stimulated. They are excellent at gathering and sharing information. They make good friends and partners, though they need mental freedom in relationships.

**Key traits:** Intelligence, adaptability, curiosity, communication skills, restlessness, versatility.
**Career:** Writing, journalism, teaching, marketing, technology, travel.

---

## Kadakam or Karka (Cancer)
**Element:** Water | **Lord:** Moon | **Friends Raashi:** Simha, Kadakam

Kadaka rashi people are deeply emotional, intuitive, and nurturing. The Moon's own sign, Kadakam makes natives highly sensitive to the feelings and moods of those around them. They are devoted to family and home, making excellent caregivers and protectors. They have a strong memory and a deep attachment to the past and their roots.

Kadaka natives are imaginative and creative, often excelling in the arts or any field that requires empathy. They can be moody, retreating into their shell when hurt, much like the crab. They are tenacious once they commit to a goal. They may be overly protective or clingy in relationships. Emotional security is paramount to them.

**Key traits:** Nurturing, intuitive, emotional, protective, tenacious, moody.
**Career:** Healthcare, social work, culinary arts, real estate, psychology, education.

---

## Simham or Simha (Leo)
**Element:** Fire | **Lord:** Sun | **Friends Raashi:** Kanya, Simham, Dhanu, Mithunam

Simha rashi people are confident, proud, and born leaders. The Sun's own sign, Simha blesses natives with a regal bearing and magnetic personality. They are generous, warm-hearted, and love to be the centre of attention. They have a strong sense of honour and integrity, and they protect those they love fiercely.

Simha natives are naturally ambitious and driven by recognition. They can be arrogant or domineering when their authority is challenged. They have a flair for drama and performance and thrive in creative and leadership roles. They are loyal friends but expect loyalty in return. They dislike being ignored or undervalued.

**Key traits:** Leadership, generosity, pride, creativity, confidence, need for recognition.
**Career:** Politics, management, entertainment, arts, military, entrepreneurship.

---

## Kanni or Kanya (Virgo)
**Element:** Earth | **Lord:** Mercury | **Friends Raashi:** Kanni, Mithuna, Thulam

Kanya rashi people are analytical, detail-oriented, and hardworking. Mercury's sign of detail and discernment, Kanya blesses natives with a sharp, discriminating intellect. They are practical and methodical, excelling in any work that requires precision and organisation. They are helpful and service-oriented, often putting others' needs before their own.

Kanya natives have high standards and can be self-critical or overly critical of others. They have a deep desire for order and cleanliness, both physically and mentally. They are modest and reserved, preferring to work quietly rather than seek the spotlight. They make excellent advisors, analysts, and craftspeople. They are reliable and conscientious in relationships but may overthink emotions.

Kanya is compatible with its own sign (Kanya/Kanni), Mithuna (Gemini — shared Mercury rulership), and Thulam (Libra — complementary intellectual nature).

**Key traits:** Analytical, diligent, modest, perfectionist, helpful, critical.
**Career:** Medicine, accounting, data analysis, editing, writing, research, nutrition.

---

## Thulam or Tula (Libra)
**Element:** Air | **Lord:** Venus | **Friends Raashi:** Thulam, Kumbam, Mithunam

Thula rashi people are balanced, charming, and deeply concerned with fairness and justice. Venus's air sign, Thula blesses natives with grace, diplomacy, and strong aesthetic sensibilities. They are social and cooperative, working best in partnerships. They have an innate sense of beauty and harmony and are drawn to the arts, design, and relationships.

Thula natives are natural peacemakers and excellent negotiators. They can be indecisive because they genuinely see all sides of a situation. They dislike conflict and confrontation. They have a strong sense of ethics and fairness. In relationships they are romantic and devoted, though they need intellectual stimulation alongside emotional connection.

**Key traits:** Diplomacy, balance, fairness, charm, indecisiveness, social intelligence.
**Career:** Law, diplomacy, design, arts, counselling, business, public relations.

---

## Vrishchikam or Vrishchika (Scorpio)
**Element:** Water | **Lord:** Mars | **Friends Raashi:** Meenam, Vrishchikam, Simham

Vrishchika rashi people are intense, perceptive, and deeply transformative. Mars's water sign, Vrishchika gives natives exceptional willpower, investigative ability, and emotional depth. They see beneath the surface of things and people, making them excellent judges of character. They are passionate and loyal to those they love, but formidable enemies to those who betray them.

Vrishchika natives are interested in mysteries, the occult, and the hidden aspects of life. They undergo major transformations during their lifetime. They can be secretive and controlling. They have a strong regenerative capacity — like the scorpion, they can rise from difficulties that would defeat others. They are intense in all pursuits.

**Key traits:** Intensity, perceptiveness, willpower, secrecy, passion, transformation.
**Career:** Research, psychology, surgery, occult sciences, investigation, finance.

---

## Dhanusu or Dhanur (Sagittarius)
**Element:** Fire | **Lord:** Jupiter | **Friends Raashi:** Dhanusu, Meenam, Simham

Dhanusu rashi people are philosophical, optimistic, and freedom-loving. Jupiter's fire sign, Dhanusu blesses natives with an expansive worldview, love of travel, and a quest for knowledge and truth. They are enthusiastic and inspiring, with a gift for teaching and storytelling. They have deep faith and a strong moral code.

Dhanusu natives are honest to the point of bluntness — they say what they think without filtering. They love adventure and dislike restrictions. They are generous and kind-hearted. They can be restless, committing to projects or relationships but then feeling the call of the horizon. They need space and freedom to thrive. They are drawn to religion, philosophy, and higher learning.

**Key traits:** Optimism, philosophy, freedom, honesty, expansiveness, bluntness.
**Career:** Teaching, law, religion, travel, publishing, international work, sports.

---

## Makaram or Makara (Capricorn)
**Element:** Earth | **Lord:** Saturn | **Friends Raashi:** Makaram, Kumbam, Thulam

Makara rashi people are ambitious, disciplined, and persistent. Saturn's earth sign, Makara blesses natives with a practical, patient, and goal-oriented nature. They believe in hard work, responsibility, and taking life seriously. They build slowly and steadily, often achieving their greatest success in the second half of life.

Makara natives are reliable, conservative, and status-conscious. They have strong organisational ability and excel in management and leadership. They can be cold, overly serious, or workaholic. They are cautious with emotions and slow to trust, but deeply loyal once committed. They respect tradition and authority and build lasting legacies.

**Key traits:** Ambition, discipline, patience, practicality, conservatism, perseverance.
**Career:** Management, finance, government, law, engineering, real estate.

---

## Kumbam or Kumba (Aquarius)
**Element:** Air | **Lord:** Saturn | **Friends Raashi:** Kumbam, Makaram, Mithunam, Thulam

Kumbha rashi people are humanitarian, independent, and intellectually innovative. Saturn's air sign, Kumbha gives natives a progressive mindset, love of humanity, and unconventional thinking. They are reformers who seek to improve society. They are friendly and sociable but value their personal freedom greatly and resist being controlled.

Kumbha natives are ahead of their time in thinking and can seem eccentric or detached. They have a wide social circle but may struggle with deep personal intimacy. They are excellent at seeing the big picture and working toward collective goals. They are idealistic and may become frustrated when society moves too slowly. They thrive in group settings and collaborative projects.

**Key traits:** Humanitarianism, independence, innovation, detachment, social consciousness.
**Career:** Technology, social reform, science, NGO work, astrology, aviation, media.

---

## Meenam or Meena (Pisces)
**Element:** Water | **Lord:** Jupiter | **Friends Raashi:** Meenam, Kadakam, Kanya, Vrishchika, Mithunam

Meena rashi people are compassionate, intuitive, and deeply spiritual. Jupiter's water sign, Meena blesses natives with empathy, imagination, and a close connection to the divine. They are gentle, adaptable, and selfless. They have vivid dreams and a rich inner life. They are natural healers and artists with a profound sensitivity to beauty and suffering.

Meena natives can be overly idealistic or escapist, sometimes retreating into fantasy to avoid harsh realities. They absorb the emotions of those around them and must guard against taking on others' burdens. They are devoted friends and deeply caring partners. They have innate spiritual wisdom that they may not fully recognise. They excel in environments that value compassion over competition.

**Key traits:** Compassion, intuition, spirituality, creativity, sensitivity, idealism.
**Career:** Arts, healing, spirituality, social work, counselling, music, film.
