# Vedic Astrology Notes

## Chart basics
The ascendant sets the frame for the whole chart. The Moon is often used for emotional patterns, while the Sun is used for identity and vitality.

## Houses
Houses describe life areas such as self, wealth, communication, home, creativity, service, relationships, transformation, learning, career, gains, and liberation.

## Reading style
Start with observable chart facts, then connect them to themes. Keep the reading structured, specific, and grounded in the chart placements.

## Learning workflow
Build a deterministic chart engine first, then add retrieval over texts, then use an LLM to synthesize the interpretation.

## Agent pattern
A strong agent flow is chart generation, rule retrieval, interpretation, and presentation.
